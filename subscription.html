❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need a subscription?

To use self-payment features (your own bank account, UPI, crypto wallets), you need an active subscription. This ensures you get full access to all features and priority support.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription anytime. Your access will continue until the end of your billing period.

What happens if my subscription expires?

If your subscription expires, you won't be able to use self-payment features. Your existing data will be safe and you can reactivate anytime.

How do I pay for subscription?

You can pay using Credit/Debit Cards, UPI, Net Banking, or Cryptocurrency. All payments are secure and encrypted.
