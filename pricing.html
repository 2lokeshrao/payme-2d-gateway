❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Gateway Instance?

A gateway instance is your own payment gateway system where you can add merchants and earn revenue from their transactions.

How do I earn money?

You charge your merchants 2% + ₹3 per transaction. Payments go directly to merchant accounts, and you earn the gateway fees.
