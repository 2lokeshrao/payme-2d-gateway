How Settlements Work

For You (Gateway Owner): You receive payments INSTANTLY when customers make purchases through your gateway. The full amount is credited to your account immediately.

For Merchants: Merchants receive their settlements after T+2 days (2 business days). You deduct your commission (2%) and transfer the remaining amount to merchants.
