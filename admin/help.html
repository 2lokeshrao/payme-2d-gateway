Frequently Asked Questions

How do I manage user accounts?
Go to Users section from the sidebar. You can view, edit, block, or delete user accounts. Use filters to search specific users.
How do I generate activation codes?
Navigate to Activation Codes section. Select the plan type, quantity, and click "Generate Codes". Codes will be created instantly.
How do I view transaction details?
Go to Transactions section. Click on any transaction to view complete details including user info, payment method, and status.
How do I configure payment methods?
Visit Payment Methods section. Toggle payment methods on/off and configure settings for each method.
How do I manage resellers?
Go to Reseller Management. You can add new resellers, set commission rates, and track their performance.
