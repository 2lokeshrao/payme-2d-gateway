How Settlement Works

T+2 Settlement Cycle:

  • T = Transaction Date
  • T+2 = Settlement Date (2 business days after transaction)
  • Weekends and holidays are excluded from business days

Gateway Charges:

  • 2% of transaction amount + ₹3 per transaction
  • Charges are automatically deducted from settlement amount

Example:

Transaction Amount: ₹1,000
Gateway Fee: (₹1,000 × 2%) + ₹3 = ₹23
Net Settlement: ₹1,000 - ₹23 = ₹977
